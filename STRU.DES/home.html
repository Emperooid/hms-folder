WHY HOLY MOUNT OF SALVATION?

Where God is Glorified, Faith is Strengthened, and Lives are Transformed

At HMS Light Church, we value spiritual growth, community, and biblical teaching. Our mission is to create an environment where individuals can encounter God's presence, grow in their faith, and witness life-changing transformations through the power of Jesus Christ. We welcome everyone to experience the love, hope, and restoration that comes from a relationship with Christ.

MINISTRIES

At Holy Mount Salvation, we believe that being a part of a Ministry Team leads to real transformation. When you join a ministry and serve others, you are able to help people encounter God, which in turn draws you closer to Him. Join a ministry today.

Evangelism ministry

Joining the Evangelism Ministry at Holy Mount of Salvation is a powerful way to share your faith and make a difference in the community. This ministry offers a unique opportunity to grow spiritually while connecting with others who are passionate about spreading the Gospel. Being part of this team means you can help bring hope to those in need and witness the transformative power of Christ in people's lives. It’s a chance to step out in faith, develop new skills, and build meaningful relationships within our church family. Together, we can spread the love and message of salvation far and wide. Join us in this important mission!

Joining the Evangelism Ministry at Holy Mount of Salvation is not just about outreach; it’s a journey of faith that deepens your connection to God and the community. In this ministry, you’ll be equipped to share the Gospel in meaningful ways, impacting lives and fostering hope. As you engage with others, you'll witness the transformative power of Christ firsthand. This ministry allows you to explore different approaches to evangelism, whether through one-on-one conversations, community events, or creative projects. It’s a chance to use your unique gifts to serve God and make a lasting impact. Additionally, being part of this ministry fosters a strong sense of belonging. You’ll work alongside like-minded individuals who share your passion for spreading love and compassion. Together, you’ll support and encourage one another, creating a vibrant community rooted in faith. If you’re ready to step out in faith and embrace this calling, we invite you to join us. Let’s work together to spread the message of salvation and be a light in our community!

service

Join us in making the world a better place. With your skills, talents and interests, we can make the body of Christ stronger and impact the world for good. To become an influential leader you must first learn to serve and the most practical way to serve others at The Holy Mount Of Salvation is to join a team. It takes all of us to make church happen so, join us today.
